Beaches/Landmarks

Playa Tamarindo

This beach is located in the south side of Puerto Rico in a town called Guayanilla. If you're looking for place to have some quality family time this is the perfect place.

Isla de Cabras

Located on the north side of the island in Palo Seco, Toa Baja. Here locals rent small cabins perfect for celebrating birthdays or any special family occasions. Perfect for chldren to run around on the beautiful white sand and enjoy the nice island breeze.

El Morro

If you are more of a history buff we assure you we have just what you are looking for. El Morro is an old fort built in San Juan 1539.Not only can you visit this fort but you can also walk around the historic town and admire the nice costal view or enjoy typical Puerto Rican food at one of it's many restaurants. Or for the kids they can enjoy kite flying or many games out on the field locted right next to El Morro.
